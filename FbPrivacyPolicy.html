Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Task Manager App ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you register or log in using Facebook, Google, Phone, or Email, we may collect your name, email address, profile picture, and phone number.
  • Device Information: Information about the device you use to access the app (such as model, OS version, and device ID) may be collected anonymously for analytics or troubleshooting.
  • Usage Data: We may collect data about how you interact with the app, such as the tasks you create, update, or delete.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate users via Firebase Authentication (Google, Facebook, Phone, Email)
  • Allow you to manage your tasks
  • Improve the app’s performance and usability
  • Respond to user queries and support requests

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only when:

  • Required by law or regulation

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC)
  • Google Sign-In

Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information:

  • Google Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as you use the app. If you delete your account or uninstall the app, your personal information may be removed from our database unless required for legal reasons.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request deletion of your data

  • Log out from the app and stop using the service at any time
